Puzzology

Vision

To expand the people' knowledge of Egyptology and Arabic History












Mission

To educate children about history in an easy and fun way, and to Increase people's awareness about the importance of Egyptian pharaonic culture, and Arabic history inside and outside Egypt, and prevent any opportunity to falsify the real history. We do that by providing the puzzle in every game store, library, and tourist bazaar.
